Overview

DxLibEx

DxLibEx is a totally open, royalty-free, DxLibrary wrapper. It has C++11/14 interface and support Windows.

DxLibrary

DxLibrary is one of famous game library, a wrapper of DirectX9/11 and Win32API. It has C99-style C++ Interface.
